Our Wonderful Bouquets

Say it with flowers and balloons - we've got you covered!

Flower Bouquets

Natural flowers have a unique and timeless beauty that can't be replicated by artificial or synthetic alternatives. They offer a sense of freshness and vitality that can brighten up any space and make people feel happy and energized.

Money Bouquets

Money bouquets can be customized for any occasion, whether it's a wedding, graduation, birthday, or other special event. We can use different types of currency, fold the bills in creative ways, and add decorative elements like flowers, ribbons, or balloons to make the bouquet even more special.

Artifical Flower Bouquets

Artificial flower bouquets don't require the same level of care and maintenance as natural flowers, which means they can last for years without wilting or fading. They also don't require regular watering or trimming, making them a convenient option for people who don't have time for regular flower care.

Birthday Decoration

Decorations are an essential part of any birthday celebration, as they help create a fun and festive atmosphere that sets the tone for the party. Whether you're decorating a home, a party venue, or a backyard, with E Bouquet there are endless ways to personalize the decorations and make the celebration extra special.

Picnic Setups

Picnic setups can be as simple or as elaborate as you want them to be. Whether you're looking for a cozy setup for a romantic picnic for two, or a larger setup for a family gathering or corporate event, there are many options available to suit your needs with us.

Balloon Arrangement

Balloon arrangements can be used for a wide range of occasions, from birthdays and weddings to corporate events and grand openings. They come in a variety of colors, shapes, and sizes, and can be customized to fit any theme or style.

About US

At E Bouquet, we're passionate about bringing joy and beauty to people's lives through flowers and balloons. Owner Kasumba Elijah has loved working with flowers and balloons since he was a child, and his hobby turned into a business when friends and family started asking him to create arrangements for their events. Today, E Bouquet is known for its stunning, one-of-a-kind bouquets and balloon arrangements that are made with care and attention to detail.
At E Bouquet, we believe in sustainability and environmental responsibility. That's why we source our flowers and balloons from local growers and suppliers whenever possible and use eco-friendly practices in our shop. We're also committed to supporting our community and giving back through partnerships with local charities.
At E Bouquet, we believe that every bouquet and balloon arrangement should be unique and tailored to the recipient's tastes. That's why we hand-select each stem and balloon and work closely with our clients to create arrangements that reflect their style and personality. Whether you want a romantic, pastel-colored bouquet or a fun and whimsical balloon sculpture, we'll work with you to make it happen.
